About the Metaverse

Imagine a virtual world where billions of people live, work, shop, learn and interact with each other -- all from the comfort of their couches in the physical world. In this world, the computer screens we use today to connect to a worldwide web of information have become portals to a 3D virtual realm that's palpable -- like real life, only bigger and better. Digital facsimiles of ourselves, or avatars, move freely from one experience to another, taking our identities and our money with us. This is known as the metaverse and we named our product as BHARATVERSE and, hype notwithstanding, it does not exist today, but we are seeing future in this Technology. We created The Project called Bharatverse which is a metaverse where people can shop, pursue education , enjoy with family and friends , work in office and do any legal task etc. This project contains Blockchains Plug-ins integrated inside which helps the avatar to do any purchase using cryptocurrency.
